The Grid product is in it’s infancy and is

When we compare the vision

We are not building a better content

or

beat Squarespace,

Wordpress or WIX.
What

A

one click, Grid users can send content


to
their site from
any digital source; photos
from an iPhone, videos

an Android


device, any piece of content from anywhere

the web. From there, our AI tools

analyzing the

the length and language of

the


preferences set for fonts, brand and layout


to deliver a site that

thousands of dollars
or hundreds of hours

and

We aren’t happy with the

the
plumbing

matter
